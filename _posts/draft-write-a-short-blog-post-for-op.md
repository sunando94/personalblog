**Agentic Workflows: A Brief Overview**

Agentic workflows refer to the set of rules, algorithms, and processes that enable an agent to interact with its environment and perform tasks. In the context of AI development, agentic workflows are crucial for creating sophisticated and adaptable agents that can learn from experience and adapt to new situations.

**Implementing Agentic Workflows**

To implement agentic workflows, you'll need to understand the concept of **Contextual Retrieval**. This involves using contextual information, such as user preferences and task requirements, to retrieve relevant data from the agent's knowledge base. By incorporating contextual retrieval into your agentic workflows, you'll be able to create more robust and flexible agents that can handle a wide range of tasks.
